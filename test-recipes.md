**Smashed Asian Cucumber Salad**
https://thewoksoflife.com/smashed-asian-cucumber-salad/

1-1.5 lbs seedless cucumbers, smash with knife into 4 sections, cut at 45° angle. Dressing: 1 tsp salt, 2.5 tsp sugar, 2 tsp sesame oil, 3 tsp light soy sauce, 1.5 tbsp rice vinegar. Add 2-4 cloves garlic (chopped), 1-2 tsp chili oil, 2 tsp toasted sesame seeds, 1/4 cup chopped cilantro. Toss and serve immediately.

**Sukuma Wiki (Kenyan Sautéed Collard Greens)**
https://www.seriouseats.com/sukuma-wiki-kenyan-sauteed-collard-greens-recipe-8637473

1 lb collard greens (or kale), remove stems, slice into thin ribbons. Heat oil, sauté 1/2 onion until softened. Add 2 diced tomatoes and 1-inch piece ginger (matchsticks), salt, cook 5min. Add collards, salt, cook 8-10min for crisp-tender or 15-25min for fully softened.

**Njahi (Kenyan Black Beans in Coconut Sauce)**
https://www.seriouseats.com/kenyan-njahi-in-coconut-sauce-5180229

2 cups njahi beans (or black beans), soak overnight in salted water. Drain, rinse, cook in fresh water until tender (1.5-4hrs). Sauté 1/2 red onion, 1/2 green bell pepper, then add garlic, scallions, ginger, 1 tsp cumin seeds. Add 3 tbsp tomato paste, 2 tsp garam masala, minced jalapeño, 1 tsp paprika, 1/2 tsp turmeric, salt, cook 2min. Add 1 can coconut milk, cooked beans, 1 cup bean cooking liquid. Simmer 30min-1hr until creamy. Stir in cilantro.
